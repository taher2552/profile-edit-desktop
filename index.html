ABOUT

Pritam Kotal, an Indian professional footballer, plays as a defender for Kerala Blasters and the India national team. Born in Uttarpara, West Bengal, Kotal's family includes a rickshaw puller and footballer Deepak Mondal. In 2020, he launched an academy for underprivileged youth footballers and donated 50,000 rupees to the West Bengal Chief Minister's fund for the COVID-19 pandemic.
